FAQs

What is a VPN?

A VPN (Virtual Private Network) encrypts your internet connection to protect your privacy and secure your online activity.

Why should I use a VPN?

A VPN helps keep your data private, protects you on public Wi-Fi, and allows access to region-restricted content.

Are VPN services legal?

VPNs are legal in most countries, but users should always follow local laws and service terms.

Can a VPN improve online security?

Yes, trusted VPN providers use encryption to enhance your online security and privacy.
