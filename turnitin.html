FAQs

What is Turnitin?

Turnitin is a leading plagiarism detection and academic integrity tool that compares submitted documents against a vast database of academic publications, websites, and student papers.

How does Turnitin check for plagiarism?

Turnitin analyzes text similarities by comparing your document with billions of online sources and academic content, then generates a similarity report highlighting matched sections.

How long does it take to receive a Turnitin report?

Most similarity reports are generated within a short time after submission, although processing times may vary depending on system demand and document size.

Does a high similarity score always mean plagiarism?

No. A similarity score indicates matched text, but it should be reviewed carefully, as properly cited references and commonly used phrases can also contribute to the score.

Can Turnitin detect AI-generated content?

Turnitin offers AI writing detection features for eligible institutions, helping educators identify content that may have been generated using AI tools.
